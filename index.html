Accessible luxury, made where it's made.

Iyaly was founded in 2022 by Damiano Fornello and co-founder Ciro, built on a simple bet: that fine perfumery, skincare, and haircare made in Italy shouldn't require a luxury-house price tag to reach people.

Every bottle and jar is produced in Italy, backed by dermatological testing on the personal-care lines and a straightforward 14-day return policy on unopened goods.
